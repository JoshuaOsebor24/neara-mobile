First steps

Getting Started

Use these guides to get comfortable with Neara from day one.

Creating an account

Open Neara, choose Sign Up, enter your details, and verify your number or email if prompted. Keep your details accurate so your account is easier to recover later.

Logging in

Use the same phone number or email you registered with. If a login attempt fails, double-check your password and your network connection before trying again.

Starting a store

Switch to store mode, complete the payment step if required, and begin entering your store details. Your store is easier to find when the name, category, and address are complete.

Completing store setup

Add your store name, category, address, phone number, cover image, and description. Complete profiles build trust and help customers understand what you sell.

Confirming location

Allow location access when prompted and confirm your store pin carefully. The map works best when the app can read your location and your store coordinates are accurate.

Adding products

Add one product at a time with a clear name, image, price, and useful category or tags. If you sell multiple sizes or versions, add variants so shoppers can choose correctly.

Chatting with stores

Open a store, tap the chat button, and send a clear message about the product you want. Including product name, quantity, and preferred pickup or delivery details speeds things up.

For sellers

Store Owner Help

Everything you need to launch, manage, and maintain your store.

Store mode payment

Complete the required store mode payment before opening your store setup tools. If payment confirmation delays, wait a moment and refresh before trying again.

Completing store details

Fill in your category, address, description, and contact number. Customers are more likely to chat or visit when your profile looks complete and credible.

Adding products

Create products with strong titles and clean images. Keep prices current and remove products that are no longer available to reduce customer confusion.

Using variants

Variants help you list different sizes, pack counts, or options under one product. Add a clear variant name and price for each option so customers can compare quickly.

Categories and tags

Choose categories that match what you actually sell. Add simple, relevant tags so your products can appear in more useful search results.

CSV product import

Use CSV import when adding many items at once. Check the column format carefully, especially product names, prices, categories, and variant fields, before uploading.

Notifications

Enable notifications so you do not miss customer chats, store updates, and important account messages. Check your phone’s app notification settings if alerts stop appearing.

Editing store information

Update your store profile any time your address, hours, phone number, or product direction changes. Keeping details fresh helps customers trust what they see.

For buyers

Customer Help

Find products, compare stores, and contact sellers with ease.

Searching for products

Use short, specific search terms for better results. Product names, categories, and popular keywords usually work better than very long phrases.

Using filters

Apply filters to narrow results by category, tags, or available options. Filters are useful when the first search returns too many results.

Viewing stores on map

Open the map to see nearby stores and select markers for a quick preview. Make sure location access is enabled if you want more accurate nearby results.

Saving stores

Save stores you want to revisit often. This makes it easier to return later without searching again.

Chatting with stores

Use chat to ask about price, stock, pickup, delivery, or product variations. Clear questions usually get faster and more helpful replies.

Fix common issues

Troubleshooting

Quick checks for the problems users ask about most often.

Map not loading

Confirm you have internet access and allow location access if Neara asks for it. If the map stays blank, close and reopen the app, then try again.

Store not appearing

Check that the store details are complete, the category is set, and the location pin is correct. Newly updated stores may also take a short moment to reflect fully.

Can’t add products

Make sure required fields like product name and price are filled. If uploads fail, check your connection and try again with a smaller image file.

Payment issues

Confirm your payment method has enough balance and that the transaction completed successfully. If money was debited without confirmation, contact support with the payment reference.

Notifications not showing

Check both Neara notification settings and your phone system settings. Low power or battery saver modes can also delay message alerts.

App not connecting

Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data to test your network. If other apps work but Neara does not, try reopening the app and signing in again.

Answers at a glance

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions from new and active users.

Do I need location access to use Neara?

Not always, but location access improves map results, nearby stores, and store pin confirmation. Without it, some map-based experiences may be limited.

How do I make my store easier to find?

Use a clear store name, correct category, complete address, good product names, relevant tags, and an accurate map location.

Why are my products not showing in search?

Check that the product name, category, and tags are filled in properly and that the product was saved successfully.

Can I edit my store details later?

Yes. You can update store information whenever your address, phone number, description, or product direction changes.

What should I send support if something breaks?

Share your account contact, device type, app version if known, screenshots if possible, and a short explanation of what you were trying to do.
